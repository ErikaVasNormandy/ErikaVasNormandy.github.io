Pocket Warp

All humans have some kind of Pocket Warp, but only Atomancers can tap into theirs at will and properly outfit their Pocket Warps.<\/p>\n

Your Namespace is where you can craft and store items, in particular the Extradimensional Components you''ll need for constructs like tools and weapons.<\/p>\n

It's almost a badge of honor for Atomancers to customize their Namespace, but try not to make it too cluttered.<\/p>\n

Usually you can't actually go into the Namespace itself without some configuration changes; for one try to make the opening big enough for people to step through.<\/p>\n

Namespaces reside in upper layers of the Void, <\/p>\n
